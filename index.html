MARKS

Migratory insect Arrival RisK prediction System

MARKS is an early-warning and risk forecasting framework for migratory insect arrivals. It integrates meteorological drivers and historical occurrence records to generate region-scale risk maps and decision-ready alerts.

Focus: cross-border invasion risk, long-distance dispersal potential, standardized model inputs/outputs, and uncertainty-aware reporting for field decision support.

Research Focus

  • Early warning of cross-border migratory insect arrival risk
  • Weather-driven long-distance dispersal and arrival probability
  • Standardized model inputs/outputs for global portability
  • Uncertainty-aware reporting for decision support
